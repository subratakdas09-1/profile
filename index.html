0000-0001-8608-3270 LinkedIn Profile ResearchGate Profile

A highly motivated scientist with an outstanding background in molecular biology and cell biology, committed to an academic and translational-research career at the forefront of biomedical science.

My research journey has taken me across continents. As a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Moran Eye Center, University of Utah (USA), I investigated angiogenesis mechanisms and developed gene therapies to prevent pathogenic neo-vascularization and ocular fibrosis. At Jeonbuk National University, South Korea, I worked on modified nucleotides for mRNA vaccine development and semisynthetic bacteria.

At IIT Kharagpur, I explored adipose-derived mesenchymal stem cells differentiation towards chondrogenesis. At Patanjali Research Institute, I studied herbal extracts and herbo-minerals in diabetes, adipogenesis, inflammation, and cancer cell proliferation. My Ph.D. at Vidyasagar University and IIT Kharagpur provided a sound foundation in Plant Molecular Biology and Plant Tissue Culture.

Today, my primary focus is phagocytic dysfunction, phagocytosis assay development, and drug repurposing — work supported by a prestigious SERB Core Research Grant from the Government of India.

Cell Biology Research Laboratory

The Cell Biology Research Laboratory is a dynamic, fully-equipped research facility dedicated to advancing our understanding of cellular mechanisms, innate immune responses, and translational biomedical applications.
